What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The new school canteen is helping for children to eat healthy and stay happy! Opening this year, the canteen features many rows of seats for students to catch up, an area to serve food,and other essentials. students can also purchase snacks and drinks to keep them going.

  • Implemented Online Attendance System

    This new, digital and real-time online system instantly tells once your child has reached school. It will tell if a child was present at school or not, also allowing seamless communication between the teacher and the parent, getting rid of human error. This new system also helps develop attendance rates to add to the child's grade.
